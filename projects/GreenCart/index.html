      About GreenCart

      GreenCart helps you shop sustainably by suggesting eco-friendly products and tracking your purchases and carbon footprint. Your cart and history are saved locally for your convenience.

      FAQ

      How are products suggested?

      We use a curated set of sustainable products. In a real app, this could be powered by a products API.

      Is my cart stored online?

      No, your cart and history are stored only in your browser.

      Can I suggest a feature?

      Yes! Please open an issue or contribute on GitHub.
