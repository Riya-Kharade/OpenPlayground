    About Mood Tunes

    Mood Tunes generates a playlist based on your current mood and lets you listen instantly via an embedded player. Your playlist history is saved locally for your convenience.

    FAQ

    How are playlists generated?

    We use a curated set of songs for each mood. In a real app, this could be powered by a music API.

    Is my data stored online?

    No, your playlist history is stored only in your browser.

    Can I suggest a feature?

    Yes! Please open an issue or contribute on GitHub.
