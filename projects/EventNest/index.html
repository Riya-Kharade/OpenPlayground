      About EventNest

      EventNest helps you discover local events and RSVP easily. Your RSVP history is saved locally for your convenience.

      FAQ

      How are events listed?

      Events are curated and can be searched by name, location, or tag.

      Is my RSVP data stored online?

      No, your RSVP history is stored only in your browser.

      Can I suggest a feature?

      Yes! Please open an issue or contribute on GitHub.
