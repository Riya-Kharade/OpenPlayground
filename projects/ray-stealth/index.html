📖 Raycasting & Lighting Manual

The Mathematics:

This engine casts hundreds of invisible mathematical rays originating from the player. It calculates the exact (x, y) intersection point where each ray hits a line segment (wall), creating a dynamic, real-time polygon that represents the player's field of view.

How to Play:

  • Movement: Use W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys to move your operative.
  • Flashlight: Use your Mouse to aim your flashlight. The darkness is absolute—you can only see what the light touches.
  • Objective: Find and collect all the glowing blue Data Drives hidden in the maze to advance to the next sector.
  • Physics: The player uses sliding collision detection to smoothly glide along walls instead of getting stuck.
