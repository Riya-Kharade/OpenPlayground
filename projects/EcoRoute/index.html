    About EcoRoute

    EcoRoute helps you find the most environmentally friendly way to travel between two locations. It considers different modes of transport and estimates the carbon footprint for each route. Your recent searches are saved locally for your convenience.

    FAQ

    How is the carbon footprint calculated?

    We use average emission factors for each mode of transport. Walking and biking have zero or near-zero emissions, while public transport and carpooling are calculated based on average occupancy and distance.

    Is my data stored online?

    No, all your route history is stored locally in your browser and never sent to a server.

    Can I suggest a feature?

    Absolutely! Please open an issue or contribute on our GitHub repository.
